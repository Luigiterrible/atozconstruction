Privacy Policy

This page explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices when using our website, forms, calls, text messaging, and email updates.

Last updated: March 13, 2026

Information We Collect

  • Contact details provided in forms, calls, text messages, WhatsApp messages, live chat, newsletter signup, and email.
  • Project details needed to prepare estimates and schedule work.
  • Basic technical data, cookie preferences, and optional analytics or chat-related information used to improve website performance and support.

How We Use Information

  • Respond to inquiries, provide estimates, and follow up about requested services.
  • Coordinate appointments, service delivery, and support.
  • Maintain service quality, compliance, communication records, and consent records when you agree to receive calls, texts, or emails.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share data with service partners, email providers, website tools, or communication platforms only when needed to operate the website, respond to your request, or provide the service you asked for.

Retention & Security

We keep records only as long as needed for operations, legal obligations, and customer service. Reasonable safeguards are applied to protect data.

Your Choices

You may request updates or removal of your contact information, unsubscribe from marketing emails, and ask us not to contact you by phone or text for future marketing communications, subject to legal and contractual requirements.

Calls, Text Messages, and Email Updates

If you submit a form and give consent, we may contact you by phone call or text message at the number you provided, including to discuss your request or provide updates. If you subscribe to our newsletter, we may send email updates, tips, and occasional promotional content. Consent is not required to purchase services, and you can opt out of future marketing communications.

Cookies, Chat, and Third-Party Tools

We use essential cookies to operate the site. Optional tools such as live chat or analytics may be activated only after you accept optional cookies through our cookie banner. Third-party services may process data under their own terms and privacy practices.

Contact for Privacy Questions

For privacy requests, unsubscribe requests, or questions about cookies and communications, contact our team directly.
